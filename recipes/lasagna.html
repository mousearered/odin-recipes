Ingredients

  • ground beef
  • cottage cheese
  • mozzarella cheese
  • parmesan cheese
  • eggs
  • lasagna noodles
  • spaghetti sauce

Steps

  1. Preheat oven.
  2. Brown ground beef in skillet over medium heat. Stir in spaghetti sauce and simmer for 5 min.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, mozarella cheese, parmesan cheese, eggs, dried parsley, salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Spread 3/4 cup of sauce on a 9x13 in baking dish. Cover with 3 uncooked lasagna noodles, 1 3/4 cupss of cheese mixture, and 1/4 cup sauce. Repeat layers. Top with remaining noodles, sauce and cheese. Add 1/2 cup water along edges of dish. Cover tightly with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake for 45 min. Uncover and bake for additional 10 min.
