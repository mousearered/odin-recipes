Ingredients

  • Elbow macaroni
  • Butter
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Cheddar cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Boil large pot of water. Add macaroni and simmer until tender. Drain and transfer to a greased baking dish.
  3. Melt 1/4 cup butter in a skillet over low heat. Whisk in flour and stir for 3 to 5 min.
  4. Gradually whisk 2 1/2 cups milk into flour mixture and bring to a simmer. Stir in cheddar and parmesan cheese. Cook until cheese is melted and sauce is thickened. Pour cheese sauce over macaroni and stir well.
  5. Bake for about 30 min.
