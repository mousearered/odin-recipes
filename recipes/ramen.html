Ingredients

  • Chicken broth
  • Dried shiitake mushrooms
  • Konbu
  • Dried bonito flakes
  • Soy sauce
  • Mirin
  • Sake
  • Water
  • Granulated sugar
  • Packed brown sugar
  • Sliced fresh ginger
  • Garlic
  • Green onions
  • Pork belly
  • Large eggs
  • Canola oil
  • Ground chili
  • Sesame seeds
  • Ramen noodles

Steps

    Make dashi

    1. Bring chicken broth to a simmer over medium heat. Remove and cool for 2 min. Add mushrooms and konbu and steep for 5 min. Add bonito and steep for 5 min.
    2. Strain and store chilled for up to 1 week.

    • Make tare and chashu

    1. Bring soy sauce, mirin, sake, water, granulated sugar, brown sugar, ginger, garlic, and green onions to a low simmer.
    2. Bring pork and 6 cups of water to simmer separately.
    3. Place pork in first pot and bring to simmer. Cook until tender, turning pork occasionally.
    4. Cool for 20 min, then strain and reserve liquid.

    • Make nitamago

    1. Bring 6 cups water to a boil in medium saucepan. Prick rounded bottoms of each egg with a pin. Lower eggs into pot and reduce heat. Simmer for 6 minutes then drain.
    2. Transfer eggs to an ice bath for 5 min. Crack eggshells gently, then return to ice bath for 10 min. Peel.
    3. Place eggs in a bowl and pour pork marinade liquid over. Let marinate in refridgerator for at least 4 hours.

    • Make garlic la-yu

    1. Simmer garlic in oil on lowest possible heat.
    2. Remove and stir in chili. Let rest 2 minutes, then stir in sesame.

    • Make ramen

    1. Cook noodles in pot of salted boiling water. Drain.
    2. Warm dashi and shiitake mushrooms over medium heat. Cook for 1 min.
    3. Add dashi, tare, and noodles to bowl. Top with chashu, nitamago, shiitake, green onion, and la-yu.
